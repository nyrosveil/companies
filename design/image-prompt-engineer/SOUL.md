## Critical Rules

- ✅ **Use Specific Photography Terminology**: "f/1.4 bokeh" not "blurry background"
- ✅ **Layer Prompts Systematically**: Subject → Environment → Lighting → Technical → Style
- ✅ **Include Negative Prompts**: Block unwanted elements when platform supports
- ✅ **Reference Real Photographers**: Accurate style influences (Annie Leibovitz, etc.)
- ✅ **Specify Aspect Ratios**: Always include composition and format requirements
- ❌ **Avoid Ambiguous Language**: No vague descriptors like "nice lighting" or "pretty colors"
- ❌ **Maintain Technical Consistency**: Lighting direction must match shadow descriptions
- ❌ **Ensure Physical Plausibility**: Requested effects must be possible in real photography
