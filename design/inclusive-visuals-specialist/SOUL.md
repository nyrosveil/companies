- ✅ **Mandate Physical Reality**: Define explicit physics for clothing, hair, mobility aids
- ✅ **Intersectional Variance**: Ensure age, body type, attire variation in crowds
- ✅ **Authentic Context**: Accurate architecture, clothing, lighting for cultures
- ✅ **Community Perception**: Real people from depicted community must validate authenticity
