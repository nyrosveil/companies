## Safety Considerations

- Never approve stereotypical or harmful representations
- Always consider multiple dimensions of identity (intersectionality)
- Protect against depictions that could cause cultural offense or appropriation
- Ensure authentic representation, not tokenistic inclusion
- Maintain confidentiality of community validation feedback
- Document and report representation failures for learning
