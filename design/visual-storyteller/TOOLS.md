## Cross-Platform Content Strategy

### Platform-Specific Optimization

#### Instagram
- **Content Types**: Stories (ephemeral), Feed (permanent), Reels (discovery)
- **Aspect Ratios**: Stories/Reels 9:16 (1080×1920), Feed 1:1 or 4:5
- **Format**: High visual impact, minimal text overlay in Reels
- **Captions**: Engaging hooks, relevant hashtags, call-to-action
- **Analytics**: Reach, impressions, saves, shares, completion rate

#### YouTube
- **Content Types**: Shorts (<60s), standard videos (2-10min), long-form (>10min)
- **Aspect Ratio**: Standard 16:9, Shorts 9:16
- **Thumbnails**: High contrast, readable text, human faces perform well
- **Titles**: Search-optimized, compelling, under 60 characters
- **Analytics**: Watch time, CTR, audience retention, subscriber growth

#### TikTok
- **Content Types**: Short-form (15-180s), trending formats, authentic feel
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- **Trend Integration**: Leverage trending sounds, effects, hashtags
- **Authenticity**: Raw, genuine content preferred over over-produced
- **Analytics**: For You Page reach, completion rate, shares, comments

#### LinkedIn
- **Content Types**: Native video, carousel posts, document shares
- **Tone**: Professional, thought leadership, business value
- **Formats**: 1280×720px minimum, carousels for step-by-step content
- **Text**: Substantive captions with professional insights
- **Analytics**: Engagement, leads, follower growth, content performance

## Narrative Design Frameworks

### Story Structure Templates

#### Hero's Journey (Classic)
1. Ordinary World: Establish normal state
2. Call to Adventure: Introduce challenge or opportunity
3. Refusal: Initial reluctance or obstacles
4. Meeting Mentor: Guide appears (brand/product as helper)
5. Crossing Threshold: Commitment to change
6. Tests & Allies: Challenges overcome with support
7. Approach: Closest approach to goal
8. Crisis/Ordeal: Major challenge or turning point
9. Reward: Achievement of goal or transformation
10. Road Back: Return to ordinary with new capability
11. Resurrection: Final test, full transformation
12. Return: New equilibrium, sharing the benefit

#### Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS)
1. Problem: Identify user's pain point clearly
2. Agitation: Explore impact and emotional weight of problem
3. Solution: Present product/service as resolution
4. Proof: Demonstrate effectiveness through evidence
5. Call to Action: Clear next step for viewer

#### Before-After-Bridge (BAB)
1. Before: Current undesirable state
2. After: Desired future state with benefits
3. Bridge: Product/service as transformation pathway

### Emotional Arc Design
- **Hope → Tension → Relief**: Classic problem-solution emotional journey
- **Curiosity → Discovery → Wonder**: Educational or revealing narrative
- **Frustration → Relief → Joy**: Pain point to solution payoff
- **Empathy → Connection → Action**: Relatable to motivated

## Visual Storytelling Patterns

### Character-Driven Stories
- **Protagonist**: User or customer as hero
- **Motivation**: Clear desire or goal driving the narrative
- **Obstacles**: Realistic challenges that create tension
- **Transformation**: Visible change demonstrating value
- **Authenticity**: Real people, genuine situations, no stereotypes

### Data-Driven Stories
- **Hook**: Surprising or counterintuitive statistic
- **Context**: What the data means in human terms
- **Visualization**: Clear chart or graphic showing pattern
- **Implication**: Why this matters to the audience
- **Call to Action**: What should be done with this insight

### Brand Narrative Consistency
- **Visual Identity**: Consistent color palette, typography, imagery style
- **Tone & Voice**: Matching written and visual communication style
- **Themes**: Recurring motifs and messaging pillars
- **Production Quality**: Consistent level of polish and professionalism
- **Emotional Resonance**: Consistent brand feeling across content

## Quality Assurance Checklist

### Technical Quality
- [ ] Resolution meets platform specifications
- [ ] Frame rate consistent throughout (24/30/60fps)
- [ ] Audio levels consistent and clear (no clipping or inaudible sections)
- [ ] Export format appropriate for platform (H.264, ProRes, etc.)
- [ ] File size optimized for delivery without quality loss
- [ ] Color accuracy verified on multiple displays

### Accessibility
- [ ] Videos have accurate captions (not auto-generated only)
- [ ] Audio descriptions provided for essential visual information
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Text in images is minimal or has text alternative
- [ ] Color contrast meets accessibility standards
- [ ] Flashing content avoided or warned

### Brand Consistency
- [ ] Color palette matches brand guidelines
- [ ] Typography follows brand standards
- [ ] Logo placement and sizing correct
- [ ] Visual style aligned with brand aesthetic
- [ ] Messaging consistent with brand voice
- [ ] Platform optimization appropriate for target audience

### Narrative Effectiveness
- [ ] Story arc clear (setup → conflict → resolution)
- [ ] Key message immediately apparent
- [ ] Emotional journey maps to intended response
- [ ] Call to action clear and compelling
- [ ] Length appropriate for platform and attention span
- [ ] First 3 seconds capture attention
