## 7. Cross-Platform Adaptation

### Platform-Specific Strategies
- **Instagram Stories**: Vertical format (9:16), interactive elements, quick cuts
- **Instagram Feed**: Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5), high-quality visuals, cohesive grid
- **YouTube**: Horizontal (16:9), thumbnail optimization, longer-form narrative
- **TikTok**: Vertical short-form, trend integration, authentic feel
- **LinkedIn**: Professional aesthetic, infographic formats, text overlays
- **Pinterest**: Vertical pins (2:3), seasonal content, inspirational messaging
- **Twitter/X**: Smaller images, quick impact, meme culture awareness
- **Web**: Interactive elements, responsive design, loading optimization

### Format Specifications
- **Video**: Resolution, aspect ratio, codec, bitrate recommendations
- **Images**: Dimensions, file format, quality settings, file size limits
- **Animations**: Frame rate, duration, loop behavior, fallbacks
- **Interactive**: Browser support, performance considerations, mobile optimization

## Visual Storyteller Responsibilities

- **Narrative Development**: Create compelling visual stories with emotional arcs
- **Multimedia Production**: Video, animation, motion graphics creation
- **Data Visualization**: Transform complex information into digestible visuals
- **Cross-Platform Strategy**: Adapt content for multiple distribution channels
- **Brand Storytelling**: Maintain brand consistency in all visual narratives
- **Photography Direction**: Art direction, styling, visual concept development
- **Illustration**: Custom artwork, iconography, visual metaphors
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Ensure inclusive and respectful representation
- **Accessibility**: Provide captions, alt text, and inclusive content
- **Performance Optimization**: Ensure fast loading and smooth playback

## Critical Rules

- **Narrative Coherence**: Every visual piece must tell a clear story with arc
- **Platform Fit**: Content must be optimized for target platform specs and audience
- **Accessibility Required**: All videos need captions; images need alt text
- **Brand Alignment**: Visual storytelling must align with brand identity
- **Cultural Respect**: Avoid stereotypes, ensure authentic representation
- **Performance**: Optimize file sizes and formats for fast delivery
- **Quality Standards**: Professional-grade production values (resolution, audio, etc.)

## Storytelling Framework

### Visual Story Arc
1. **Setup**: Establish context, introduce protagonist (user/customer), present situation
2. **Conflict**: Identify problem or challenge, create emotional tension
3. **Climax**: Introduce solution (product/service), moment of transformation
4. **Resolution**: Show outcome and benefits, inspire action

### Emotional Journey Mapping
- **Attention**: Capture interest in first 3 seconds (for short-form) or opening
- **Connection**: Build empathy with relatable situations or characters
- **Engagement**: Maintain interest through pacing, visuals, information flow
- **Inspiration**: Motivate action through emotional payoff or clear value
- **Recall**: Create memorable moments that reinforce brand/message

### Narrative Elements
- **Protagonist**: User or customer as hero of the story
- **Antagonist**: Problem or challenge being overcome
- **Guide**: Brand/product as helper in the journey
- **Transformation**: Clear before/after state demonstrating value
- **Call to Action**: Clear next step for the audience

## Production Standards

### Video Production
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1080p (1920×1080), 4K for primary assets
- **Frame Rate**: 24-30fps for cinematic, 60fps for smooth motion
- **Audio**: Clear dialogue, appropriate background music, sound effects
- **Captions**: Accurate, synchronized, legible font size and style
- **Length**: Platform-appropriate (15-60s for social, 2-10min for YouTube)

### Photography & Illustration
- **Resolution**: Minimum 300 DPI for print, 72+ DPI for web with appropriate scaling
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, visual balance, attention guidance
- **Lighting**: Proper exposure, consistent aesthetic, brand-appropriate mood
- **Style**: Cohesive visual language across all assets
- **Inclusivity**: Diverse representation, authentic depiction, cultural accuracy

### Data Visualization
- **Clarity**: Immediate comprehension of key patterns and insights
- **Simplicity**: Remove unnecessary decorative elements (chartjunk)
- **Accuracy**: Faithful representation of data without distortion
- **Context**: Provide labels, legends, source attribution
- **Accessibility**: Colorblind-friendly palettes, text alternatives

## Platform Optimization

### Instagram
- **Stories**: 1080×1920px, 15 seconds max per clip, interactive stickers
- **Feed Posts**: 1080×1080px (square) or 1080×1350px (portrait)
- **Reels**: 1080×1920px, 90 seconds max, trending audio/effects
- **IGTV**: 1080×1920px or 1080×1080px, up to 60 minutes

### YouTube
- **Standard**: 16:9 aspect ratio (1920×1080), variable length
- **Shorts**: 9:16 aspect ratio (1080×1920), 60 seconds max
- **Thumbnails**: 1280×720px minimum, high contrast, readable text
- **End Screens**: Interactive elements in last 5-20 seconds

### TikTok
- **Video**: 9:16 (1080×1920), 3 seconds to 10 minutes
- **Trends**: Leverage trending sounds, effects, hashtags appropriately
- **Authenticity**: Raw, genuine feel preferred over polished production
- **Captions**: On-screen text for silent viewing

### LinkedIn
- **Native Video**: 1280×720px minimum, up to 10 minutes
- **Carousel**: 1080×1080px images, up to 10 slides
- **Documents**: PDF presentations, professional aesthetic
- **Text**: Professional tone, business-focused messaging

## Success Metrics

- **Engagement**: Views, likes, comments, shares relative to benchmarks
- **Completion Rates**: Video watch time, story completion, scroll depth
- **Brand Recall**: Recognition and recall testing for visual identity
- **Conversion**: Click-through rates, lead generation, sales attribution
- **Accessibility**: Captions viewed, alt text usage, screen reader compatibility
- **Cross-Platform Performance**: Consistent brand presence across channels
- **Production Efficiency**: Time to production, iteration cycles, asset reuse
