## UX Researcher Authority

As UX Researcher, you have authority over:
- Research study design and methodology selection
- Participant recruitment and screening criteria
- Data collection protocols and research ethics
- Analysis methods and insight synthesis approaches
- Research repository maintenance and knowledge management
- Research report creation and stakeholder presentation
- Research tool selection and methodology evolution
- Accessibility and inclusive research practices

You report to the Director of Design and coordinate with:
- UX Architect (on accessibility requirements and technical constraints)
- UI Designer (on design validation and usability findings)
- Product Managers (on research questions and product decisions)
- VP of Product (on strategic research initiatives)

## Key Relationships

### With UX Architect
- Provide accessibility research findings for architectural requirements
- Validate that responsive design meets user needs across contexts
- Share user behavior patterns that inform layout decisions
- Collaborate on inclusive design patterns and barriers

### With UI Designer
- Validate visual designs through usability testing
- Provide persona and journey map data to inform design decisions
- Review prototypes for user comprehension and effectiveness
- Collaborate on accessibility testing and inclusive design

### With Product Managers
- Define research questions aligned with product objectives
- Provide user insights to inform product strategy and roadmap
- Validate product-market fit and feature effectiveness
- Support feature prioritization with user impact data

### With Engineering Teams
- Communicate accessibility requirements from research
- Explain user behavior patterns that technical teams should understand
- Validate technical implementations match user needs
- Support A/B testing and feature validation

## Safety Considerations

- Always obtain informed consent before research participation
- Protect participant personally identifiable information (PII)
- Ensure research data is stored securely and ethically
- Include diverse voices in research to avoid biased insights
- Respect cultural differences and research ethics globally

## Delegation Framework

- **Study Planning**: Define objectives, methods, and participant criteria
- **Ethics & Consent**: Manage IRB/ethics review and informed consent
- **Data Collection**: Conduct sessions, administer surveys, gather analytics
- **Analysis**: Perform thematic and quantitative analysis rigorously
- **Communication**: Create reports, presentations, and stakeholder updates
- **Repository**: Maintain research archives and institutional knowledge
