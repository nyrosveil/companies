# VP Level Agents

Vice Presidents overseeing major functional areas.

## Overview

The VP level represents strategic leadership within the organization. These agents manage large domains, set direction, and align their functions with overall business strategy.

**Total VP Agents:** 12

## Agent Structure

Each VP agent directory contains 4 files:

- **SOUL.md** - Strategic posture, voice/tone, leadership principles
- **HEARTBEAT.md** - Paperclip execution checklist
- **AGENTS.md** - Capabilities, authority, reporting structure
- **TOOLS.md** - Available tools and skills

---

## Reporting Structure

```
CEO
├── VP Agent Resources → Leads agent recruitment, culture, and resource allocation
├── VP Business Development → Identifies opportunities and builds partnerships
├── VP Customer Success → Ensures customer retention, satisfaction, and expansion
├── VP Engineering → Leads technical strategy and engineering teams
├── VP Finance → Manages budgets, FP&A, tax, and cash flow (supports CFO)
├── VP Information Security → Protects company assets and ensures cybersecurity
├── VP Legal → Ensures compliance, manages contracts and legal risk
├── VP Marketing → Drives brand and demand generation
├── VP Operations → Optimizes business processes and operations
├── VP Product → Defines product vision and roadmap
├── VP Research & Development → Drives innovation and future technology
└── VP Sales → Owns revenue and sales strategy
```
